# MMSFM Manuscript Writing

Use this skill for `manuscript/main.tex` and nearby publication prose when the passage must match what is actually defined, proved, implemented, or measured.

1. Read the exact target passage with one to two surrounding paragraphs and any nearby equations or citations.
2. State the local job of the passage in one sentence.
3. Rewrite once that job is clear.
4. Put the claim before the rhetoric.
5. Cut roadmap, defensive, and reviewer-facing prose unless it is truly needed.
6. Preserve notation, equation labels, and citations unless the task requires a real correction.
7. Do not normalize degradation handling, fallback procedures, hacks, heuristics, local stabilizations, or post-processing bandages as if they were faithful general algorithms.
8. Re-read the edited passage against the previous and next paragraph.

Use SIAM as house style. Treat Higham only as a light readability guide.
